# JiTpro Pitch Deck

---

## 1. Problem

Construction projects are unstable before they begin.

- Design and specifications are incomplete at award
- Procurement constraints are not identified or sequenced
- Responsibility for missing information is undefined
- Decision deadlines do not exist
- Contractors commit to schedules without procurement clarity.

**Failure Mechanism:**
Unresolved procurement constraints collide with a fixed schedule

**Result:**
- Reactive procurement
- Missed lead times
- Margin compression
- Loss of credibility

---

## 2. Solution

JiTpro establishes procurement control before mobilization.

- Identifies scope gaps and missing design/specification
- Converts unknowns into tracked constraints
- Assigns responsibility for resolution
- Aligns decisions with procurement deadlines
- Sequences all materials to required on-site dates

**Output:**
A structured procurement execution plan tied to real constraints.
